What is Email Marketing ?

Email marketing is the sending of a commercial message to many people through email. For example, when you visit a website, you get a subscription option through email. When you enter your email and subscribe to that website, then your email address gets stored in the database of that website or company. Then whenever any new update comes in the company or website, its email is immediately sent in your mail.

How to start Email Marketing ?

If you also want to start email marketing, then for this you must first have a list of email addresses. You can also purchase email marketing lists from various companies who sell this type of data for email marketing.

These are following steps by which you can start email marketing :-

  • Select an Email Marketing Service Provider
  • Select a template for Email Marketing
  • Add Email text
  • Submit Email Marketing List
  • Start Email Marketing Campaign

When you have an email marketing list, then you have to choose the email marketing service provider (ESP). There are various email marketing service provider tools which can be used for email marketing. For example :- getresponse, aweber, hubspot, constant contact, benchmark email etc.

Now Select the template for email marketing campaign. After that you have to write the message whatever you want to send through email.

After writing the text in the template, you have to submit the email marketing list which contains all the email addresses. In this way you can start an email marketing campaign very easily.
