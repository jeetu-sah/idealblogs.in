>Hollywood Movies are best known for its Action, thriller and excellent scripts. Hollywood Movies are famous around the world. It is the world's largest box office gross revenue industry. Here we are going to discuss the most successful Hollywood Movies which have the highest collection worldwide of all time.

List of Top 5 Hollywood Movies

1. Avengers:Endgame

Avengers : Endgame is an American superhero based Hollywood movie which was released in 2019. Avengers : Endgame was the sequel of Avengers : Infinity War (2018). The movie shattered the record of largest opening weekend of all time Worldwide. The movie grossed $2,797,800,564 worldwide.

2. Avatar

Avatar is an epic science fiction Hollywood movie which was released in 2009. The movie is still in the second place in top grossed movies worldwide. Sam Worthington and Zoe Saldana starring Avatar grossed $2,790,439,000 worldwide.

3. Titanic

Titanic is an American epic romantic Hollywood film which is based on a real story of disaster. Titanic was released in 1997. Leonardo Dicarpio and Katewinslet starring Titanic grossed $2,195,169,696 worldwide.

4. Star War: The Force Awakens

Star War: The Force Awakens is a Hollywood movie which was released in 2015. John Boyega, Daisy Ridley, Harrison Ford, Adam Driver, Oscar Isaac,and Carrie Fisher starring in Star Wars: The Force Awakens grossed $2,068,223,624 worldwide.

5. Avengers : Infinity War

Avengers : Infinity War is an American superhero based Hollywood Movie which was released in 2018. Marvel's Avengers : Infinity War is based on the Marvel comics superhero team known as Avengers. Tom Holland starring Avengers : Infinity War grossed $2,048,359,754 worldwide and the movie shattered the record of highest-grossing domestic opening weekend ever.
