How to Register an account on Godaddy ?

Godaddy is the most trusted website to buy domain names. To buy a domain from Godaddy, you must first create an account or if you already have an account you can login simply. For new Users, You must follow these steps :

Step 1 : Click on 'Create An Account'.

Step 2 : You can create an account with google or facebook or you can also create with email. You must provide a correct email.

Step 3 : For the verification of email you must provide 4-digit verification which have been sent to you in email and if you want to contact customer care then they ask you for an account pin.

Step 4 : After verification of account, Select 'Payment Information' option and then select the option 'Continue'.

Step 5 : Now you must fill your debit card/credit card details and then click on 'Place Your Order'. After the completion of Payment you will get your domain details.

Step 6 : To see your domain, click on your profile and here click on 'Manage My Domains'. Now you can post blogs on your website.

How to buy Domain from Godaddy ?

Step 1 : Open any web browser on your Mobile or PC and type url https://in.godaddy.com/

Step 2 : Now you will find a search box for searching domain names.

Step 3 : Enter a domain name whatever you want to choose for your website and search here and click on 'Search Domain'.

Step 4 : If the domain is available then it will show a message 'Yes! your domain is available buy it before someone else does.'

Step 5 : On the right side of Domain, there will be a price and 'Select' option given. If you want another domain extension such as .in, .org, .net then you can buy it from the suggestion section. You can also buy multiple domain extensions.

Step 6 : After Selecting domain name click on 'Continue to Cart'.
